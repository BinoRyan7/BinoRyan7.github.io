Flex Box: Justify-content vs. Align-items vs. Align content
Justify Content: aligns and spaces items along the flex axis. (x-axis with display: flex)
Justify Content: flex-start
Justify Content: flex-end
Justify Content: center
Justify Content: space around
Justify Content: space between
Align Items: aligns items along the cross axis. (y-axis with display: flex)
Align Items: flex-start
Align Items: flex-end
Align Items: center
Align Content: this controls the spacing between rows. These next sections will have 10 flex items in multiple rows.
Align Content: flex-start
Align Content: flex-end
Align Content: center
Align Content: space around
Align Content: space between
