Works of Vincent Van Gogh

The Starry Night

Vincent van gogh painted Starry Night in 1889 during his stay at the asylum of Saint-Paul-de-Mausile near Saint-Remy-de-Pronvece. Van Gough lived wll in the hospital; he was allowd more freedoms than any of the other patients. If attended, he could leave the hospital grounds; he was allowed to paint, read, and withdraw into his own room.

Sunflowers

Van Gogh's paintings of Sunflowers are among his most famous. He did them in Arles, in the south of France, in 188 and 1889. Vincent painted a total of five large canvases with sunflowers in a vase, with three shades of yellow 'and nothing else'. In this way, he deomnstrated that it was possiible to create an image with numerous variationsn of a single color, without any loss of eloquence.
